# Deep Cleaning Services

Intensive cleaning service for a thorough and detailed clean of every corner of your space. Perfect for spring cleaning, move-in/move-out, or homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while.

## What Makes Deep Cleaning Different?

Unlike regular cleaning, deep cleaning reaches every hidden spot and neglected area:

- **Behind and under all furniture and appliances**
- **Inside all cabinets, drawers, and closets**
- **Baseboards, door frames, and window tracks**
- **Light fixtures, ceiling fans, and air vents**
- **Grout and tile deep cleaning**
- **Appliance interiors (oven, fridge, microwave)**
- **Blinds and window treatments**
- **Upholstery and mattress cleaning**

## When to Schedule Deep Cleaning

- **Spring Cleaning**: Annual refresh of your entire home
- **Move-In / Move-Out**: Ensure the space is spotless
- **After Renovation**: Remove construction dust and debris
- **Before Special Events**: Prepare for holidays or gatherings
- **Regular Deep Clean**: Every 3-6 months for maintenance
