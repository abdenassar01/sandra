# Post-Construction Cleaning

Specialized cleaning services after construction or renovation projects. We handle the final cleanup so you can enjoy your newly renovated or built space immediately.

## Final Touch-Ups for Your Project

Our post-construction cleaning ensures your space is move-in ready:

- **Removal of all construction debris**
- **Dust removal from all surfaces**
- **Window cleaning (interior and exterior)**
- **Floor cleaning and polishing**
- **Fixture and hardware cleaning**
- **Vent and duct cleaning**
- **Paint and adhesive residue removal**
- **Final inspection and touch-ups**

## What We Clean After Construction

### Rough Cleaning (During Construction)
- Remove large debris and materials
- Sweep and vacuum construction dust
- Clean windows and fixtures
- Prepare surfaces for final cleaning

### Final Cleaning (After Construction)
- Detailed dusting of all surfaces
- Sanitization of bathrooms and kitchens
- Floor cleaning and polishing
- Window and glass cleaning
- Touch-up paint removal from fixtures
- HVAC vent cleaning

## Types of Projects We Service

1. New Home Construction - ready for handover to homeowners
2. Home Renovations - kitchens, bathrooms, additions
3. Commercial Build-Outs - office spaces, retail locations
4. Remodeling Projects - any scope or size

## Why Professional Post-Construction Cleaning?

"Construction dust and debris can be hazardous and difficult to remove completely. Professional cleaning ensures safety and cleanliness."

Our specialized post-construction cleaning removes fine dust that regular vacuums miss, eliminates construction debris safely, protects new surfaces from damage, and ensures proper cleanup of hazardous materials.

- HEPA vacuums for fine dust
- Industrial cleaning solutions
- Specialized tools for adhesive removal
- Floor polishing and buffing machines
